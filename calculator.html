Good to know

What this calculator doesn't include

  • ✓

    CMHC / default insurance premium

    Required for down payments under 20%, typically added to the mortgage principal.

  • ✓

    Property tax & utilities

    Often collected with your payment but not part of the mortgage itself.

  • ✓

    Closing costs

    Legal fees, land transfer tax, and inspection costs are paid separately at closing.

Rule of thumb

Accelerated bi-weekly pays your mortgage off faster

Accelerated bi-weekly payments equal one extra monthly payment per year, which can shave years off a typical amortization without a noticeable change to your monthly budget.
