Built to sit on your side of the table

BestRate Mortgages exists because comparing mortgage rates in Canada used to mean calling ten banks yourself. We built the desk we wished existed.

Our mission

Make the best rate the easy rate

Most people spend more time comparing flights for a weekend trip than they do comparing the mortgage they'll pay for the next five years. Not because the stakes are low — the opposite — but because comparing lenders has always been slow, repetitive, and opaque.

Our mission is simple: put every major lender's rate in one place, match each client with the option that actually fits their situation, and let a licensed broker do the negotiating — without charging the homeowner a cent to do it.

What we stand for

Three things we don't compromise on

01

Whole-of-market, always

We're not owned by a bank and we don't push one lender over another. Every recommendation is ranked on what's actually best for the client's file.

02

Plain-language advice

Mortgage terms are confusing on purpose sometimes. We explain trade-offs in plain terms before you ever sign anything.

03

Licensed, accountable brokers

Every broker connected through our platform is licensed in their province and held to a duty of care to the client — not the lender.
